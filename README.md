# vr-one2twenty

> 日本語のREADMEはこちらです: [README.ja.md](README.ja.md)

A fast-paced VR/AR game that challenges your speed and accuracy. The goal is to touch targets in sequential order as quickly as possible.

- **Play "One to Twenty" (VR/AR):** [https://code4fukui.github.io/vr-one2twenty/](https://code4fukui.github.io/vr-one2twenty/)
- **Play "One to Seventeen SDGs" (VR):** [https://code4fukui.github.io/vr-one2twenty/sdgs.html](https://code4fukui.github.io/vr-one2twenty/sdgs.html)

## Features

- **Immersive Gameplay:** Full VR and AR support using WebXR for an engaging experience.
- **Two Game Modes:**
    - **One to Twenty:** Touch numbered spheres from 1 to 20 in the correct order.
    - **SDGs Version:** An educational variant where you touch the 17 Sustainable Development Goal icons in sequence.
- **Performance Tracking:** Saves your best time in your browser's local storage to compete against yourself.
- **Interactive Controls:** Designed for VR controllers, responding to player movements and trigger presses.
- **Audio Feedback:** Includes sound effects for starting the game, hitting targets, and clearing the level.

## Built With

- [Three.js](https://threejs.org/) - For the core VR/AR experience.
- [A-Frame](https://aframe.io/) - For the SDGs VR version.
